Define a matrix A and calculate dr and frequency from the eigenvalues

A=[0      1
  -1.01  -.2];
lam=eig(A)
w=imag(lam(1))
sig=real(lam(1))
freq=w/2/pi
dr=exp(sig/freq)

lam =

  -0.1000 + 1.0000i
  -0.1000 - 1.0000i


w =

    1.0000


sig =

   -0.1000


freq =

    0.1592


dr =

    0.5335

Exercise 9.2 Calculate the time domain response

x0=[1;-.1];
dt=0.001;
t=1:dt:10;
X=zeros(2,length(t));
X(:,1)=x0;
for i=2:length(t)
    X(:,i)=X(:,i-1)+dt*A*X(:,i-1);
end
plot(t,X(1,:))
hold on
plot(t,X(2,:),'r');
